Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a contact?

Tap the Contacts tab, then tap the + button in the top right. Fill in as much or as little detail as you want — only a first or last name is required.

How do I log an interaction?

Open any contact's detail page and tap "Log Interaction." Choose the date, type (call, text, email, etc.), how it went, and add any notes. The interaction will appear in their timeline.

What do the health colors mean?

Green (Active): You're staying connected — frequent recent interactions.
Yellow (Fading): It's been a while — consider reaching out.
Red (Neglected): This relationship needs attention — it's been too long.

How do follow-up reminders work?

Set a "follow up by" date on any contact. Overdue follow-ups appear on your Dashboard with a red indicator. You can set quick follow-ups (1 week, 2 weeks, 1 month) from any contact's detail page.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free lets you track up to 25 contacts with basic interaction logging. Pro unlocks unlimited contacts, groups and tags, relationship insights, smart reminders, CSV export, and advanced search. Pro starts at $4.99/month or $39.99/year with a 7-day free trial.

Where is my data stored?

Everything is stored locally on your device. We don't have servers or cloud storage. Your personal data never leaves your phone unless you export it.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → InnerCircle Pro → Cancel. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I export my contacts?

Pro subscribers can go to Settings → Export Contacts (CSV). This creates a CSV file you can open in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet app.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

How do I delete all my data?

Since all data is stored locally, deleting the InnerCircle app from your device removes all data permanently.
