Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

InnerCircle ("we", "our", "the app") is developed by StackStats Apps LLC. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data InnerCircle handles and how.

Data Storage

All your data — contacts, interactions, groups, notes, and settings — is stored locally on your device only. InnerCircle does not have a server, cloud database, or backend service. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use advertising or ad tracking
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • We do not access your device contacts, photos, or other apps

Subscriptions

InnerCircle Pro subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. Subscription management (cancellation, renewal) is handled through your Apple ID settings.

CSV Export

Pro subscribers can export their contact data as a CSV file. This export is generated locally on your device and shared through iOS's standard share sheet. We have no access to exported files.

Third-Party Services

InnerCircle uses no third-party SDKs, analytics services, crash reporters, or advertising networks. The only external service interaction is Apple's StoreKit for subscription processing.

Children's Privacy

InnerCircle does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app stores only data that users manually enter.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all InnerCircle data by deleting the app. No server-side data exists to delete.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated effective date at the top of this page.
